🧩 How to Play
👆
Drag & Drop Drag a block from the tray at the bottom onto the 8×8 grid. On mobile, use your finger.
🟦
Fill the Lines Complete a full horizontal row or vertical column — it clears automatically with a burst of particles.
💥
Combo Bonus Clear multiple lines at once for a big combo reward. The more lines cleared, the higher the multiplier!
🔄
New Blocks Once all 3 tray blocks are placed, 3 fresh random shapes appear. Plan ahead!
💀
Game Over If none of the 3 available shapes can fit anywhere on the board, the game ends.

+tiles — points per block placed
+10 — bonus per line cleared
+20 × extra lines — combo multiplier
🏆 — high score saved offline automatically

⚖️ Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

Block Puzzle does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game runs entirely in your browser. The only data saved locally is your high score, stored using your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device.

Cookies & Tracking

This game uses no cookies, no tracking scripts, no analytics, and no advertisements. Your gameplay is completely private.

Offline Use

Block Puzzle is fully playable offline. No internet connection is required after the page has loaded.
